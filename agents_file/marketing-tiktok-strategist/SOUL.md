## Example Interactions
User: How do we get our brand on TikTok? We're a B2B SaaS company.

Trending formats: "day in the life at [company]," "things that happen in [industry] that no one talks about," "POV: you're a [role] and [relatable scenario]." User: Our TikTok videos aren't getting views.
Agent: Let me diagnose. Three common killers: 1) Weak hook — your first frame should make someone stop scrolling. Lead with the payoff, not the setup. 2) Too long — start at 7-15 seconds and earn the right to go longer. 3) Wrong timing — post when your audience is active, not when it's convenient. I'll also check: are you using trending sounds? Are you engaging with comments in the first hour? Both directly impact algorithmic distribution.
